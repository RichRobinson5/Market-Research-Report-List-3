<p><p>Stationary Li-ion battery market is divided into three main types based on their cathode materials: Li-Ni (lithium nickel), Li-Ni-Co (lithium nickel cobalt), and Li-Mn (lithium manganese). Li-Ni batteries offer high energy density, Li-Ni-Co batteries provide a good balance between energy density and power output, while Li-Mn batteries are known for their safety and stability. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in stationary energy storage systems, catering to a wide range of needs in the market.</p></p>

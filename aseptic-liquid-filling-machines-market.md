<p><p>Aseptic liquid filling machines come in two main types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic machines are designed to handle the entire filling process without the need for human intervention, from container handling to sealing. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual input from operators, such as loading containers or adjusting settings. Both types cater to different production needs and scale, with fully-automatic machines being suitable for high-volume production while semi-automatic machines are commonly used in smaller scale operations.</p></p>

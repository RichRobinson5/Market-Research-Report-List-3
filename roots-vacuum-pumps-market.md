<p><p>Roots vacuum pumps are commonly used in various industrial applications to create a low-pressure environment. Single-stage Roots vacuum pumps consist of a pair of rotating lobes that continuously trap and transport gas, providing a moderate level of vacuum. On the other hand, multi-stage Roots vacuum pumps employ multiple sets of lobes, increasing the compression ratio and allowing for a higher level of vacuum. Both types play a crucial role in different industries by minimizing air contamination, enhancing process efficiency, and enabling stable operations.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>In-Line RPVs</li><li>Off-Line RPVs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residual Pressure Valves (RPVs) are devices used in hydraulic systems to maintain a specified pressure when the system is not in use. In-Line RPVs are installed directly in the hydraulic line to regulate pressure during system shutdown, preventing pressure loss and potential leakage. Off-Line RPVs, on the other hand, are installed externally to the hydraulic system and provide pressure control when the main system is turned off. Both types of RPVs are vital in preventing hydraulic system damage, ensuring safety, and improving overall system efficiency.</p></p>

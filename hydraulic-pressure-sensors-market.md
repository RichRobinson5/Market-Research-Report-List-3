<p><ul><li>Differential Type</li><li>Relative Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic pressure sensors are classified into two main types: differential type and relative type. Differential type sensors measure the pressure difference between two points in a system, providing accurate readings in applications such as flow measurement. On the other hand, relative type sensors measure pressure relative to a reference point, making them suitable for various applications where precise pressure readings are required. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation.</p></p>

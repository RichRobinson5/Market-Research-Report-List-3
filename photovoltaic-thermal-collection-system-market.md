<p><p>Photovoltaic Thermal (PVT) collection systems use solar energy to produce electricity and heat simultaneously. The market offers three main types: flat plate collectors, vacuum tube collectors, and others. Flat plate collectors consist of an insulated box with a dark absorber plate covered by a transparent glazing to trap solar radiation. Vacuum tube collectors consist of rows of parallel tubes containing an absorber. Other collectors include innovative designs that combine PV cells and thermal absorbers. These systems are designed to maximize energy efficiency and reduce the reliance on traditional energy sources.</p></p>

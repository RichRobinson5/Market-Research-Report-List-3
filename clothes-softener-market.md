<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Soap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clothes softener market is divided into two major types: liquid softeners and soap softeners. Liquid softeners are formulated as a liquid that can be added to the washing machine during the rinse cycle. They work by coating the fibers of the clothes, making them feel smoother and reducing static cling. On the other hand, soap softeners are typically solid bars or pellets that are added directly to the washing machine. They dissolve during the rinse cycle and provide the same softening effects as liquid softeners. Both types are popular choices in the market for achieving soft and comfortable clothes.</p></p>

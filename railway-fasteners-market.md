<p><p>The railway fasteners market can be classified into two main types: bolt type and non-bolt type. Bolt-type fasteners primarily include rail bolts, fish bolts, and anchor bolts, which are used to connect and secure rails and other components of the railway track system. Non-bolt type fasteners, on the other hand, encompass rail clips, rail pads, and rail shoulders. These non-bolt type fasteners provide stability and ensure proper alignment of rails while absorbing vibrations and reducing noise generated by train operations.</p></p>

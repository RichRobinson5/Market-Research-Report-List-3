<p><ul><li>Built-In Ballast</li><li>External Ballast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Off-road vehicle HID lamps with built-in ballast have the ballast integrated into the lamp housing, eliminating the need for an external ballast. This type of HID lamp is more compact and easier to install. On the other hand, off-road vehicle HID lamps with external ballast require a separate ballast unit to be mounted elsewhere in the vehicle. While they may be slightly more complicated to install, external ballast HID lamps are typically easier to replace or repair if necessary.</p></p>

<p><p>The automatic capsule machine market includes two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully automatic capsule machines are equipped with advanced technology and can automatically complete all stages of the capsule filling process, from filling to sealing, with minimal human intervention. On the other hand, semi-automatic capsule machines require some manual effort to complete certain stages, such as filling and sealing. While fully automatic machines offer convenience and efficiency, semi-automatic machines may be more suitable for smaller-scale operations or those with specific customization requirements.</p></p>

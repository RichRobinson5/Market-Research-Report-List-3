<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The LAN Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layer 2 Switching</li><li>Layer 3 Switching</li><li>Layer 4 Switching</li><li>Multi-Layer switching (MLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>- Layer 2 Switching: Operates at the data link layer and forwards data based on MAC addresses.</p><p>- Layer 3 Switching: Operates at the network layer and makes forwarding decisions based on IP addresses.</p><p>- Layer 4 Switching: Operates at the transport layer and considers port numbers in addition to IP addresses.</p><p>- Multi-Layer Switching (MLS): Integrates functionalities of layer 2, 3, and sometimes layer 4 switching to provide more efficient and scalable network solutions. Each type of switching offers different levels of intelligence and functionality to meet specific networking needs.</p></p>

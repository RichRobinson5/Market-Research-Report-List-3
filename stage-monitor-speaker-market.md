<p><p>The stage monitor speaker market consists of two types: active monitor speakers and passive monitor speakers. Active monitor speakers have built-in amplifiers, allowing them to receive a line-level audio signal and amplify it. They are usually compact and easy to set up, making them more convenient for musicians and live performances. Conversely, passive monitor speakers require an external amplifier to power them. They offer greater flexibility in amplifier choice and are commonly used in professional settings where specific sound requirements dictate the preferred amplifier.</p></p>

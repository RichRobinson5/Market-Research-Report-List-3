<p><p>Continuous Rotary Extrusion Equipment Market has two main types based on the shaping method: Curvilinear Type Continuous Extrusion Machine and Radial Type Continuous Extrusion Machine. The Curvilinear Type utilizes a curved die to shape the material, suitable for producing hollow tubes or profiles. On the other hand, the Radial Type uses a radial die to give a circular shape to the material, ideal for producing solid rods or wires. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in the manufacturing industry for efficient continuous extrusion processes.</p></p>

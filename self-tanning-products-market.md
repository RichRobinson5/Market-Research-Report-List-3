The result is a sun-kissed glow that lasts for several days.</p><p>The self-tanning products market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue this trend in the future. Factors driving the growth of this market include the increasing awareness about the harmful effects of UV radiation on the skin, growing concerns about skin cancer, and the desire for a tanned appearance all year round.</p><p>Furthermore, the self-tanning products market has benefited from advancements in formulation technology, resulting in the development of products that offer a more natural-looking tan, streak-free application, and longer-lasting results.

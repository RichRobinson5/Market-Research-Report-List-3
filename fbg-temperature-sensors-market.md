<p><p>FBG temperature sensors are used for measuring temperature in various applications. There are two types of FBG temperature sensors: Distributed Sensing and Fiber Optic Point Sensing. </p><p>Distributed Sensing involves the use of multiple sensors along the length of the fiber optic cable to continuously measure temperature at different points. This type of sensor provides a real-time temperature profile along the entire length of the fiber.</p><p>Fiber Optic Point Sensing, on the other hand, uses a single FBG sensor to measure temperature at a specific point. This type of sensor is commonly used in applications where temperature monitoring at a specific location is required, such as industrial machinery or environmental monitoring.</p></p>

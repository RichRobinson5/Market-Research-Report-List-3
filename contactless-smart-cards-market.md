RFID cards use radio frequency identification technology to wirelessly communicate with card readers, making them useful for public transport or access control. RFIC cards operate similarly, but with a focus on identification, authentication, and tracking applications. RFCPU cards, on the other hand, combine the features of RFID and microprocessor technology, enabling advanced functionalities like storing and processing data on the card itself. Overall, these cards provide convenient, secure, and efficient means of payment and identification.</p></p>

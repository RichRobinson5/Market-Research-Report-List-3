Conducting polymers (CP) utilize organic polymers that change their electrical conductivity when exposed to different odors. Quartz crystal microbalance (QCM) uses a quartz crystal resonator that vibrates at different frequencies when subjected to different chemicals, enabling odor identification. Additionally, there are other emerging technologies in the market that offer alternative means of odor detection and analysis.</p></p>

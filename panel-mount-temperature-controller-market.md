<p><ul><li>Switch Controller</li><li>Proportional Controller</li><li>Pid Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Panel mount temperature controllers are used in various industries to regulate and control temperature. Switch controllers are simple on/off devices that activate or deactivate heat sources based on temperature setpoints. Proportional controllers adjust the heat source's power output based on the difference between the actual and desired temperatures. PID controllers employ advanced algorithms to continuously monitor and adjust the heat source to maintain precise temperature control. These controllers are widely used in industrial processes such as HVAC systems, food processing, and manufacturing to ensure accurate and stable temperature control.</p></p>

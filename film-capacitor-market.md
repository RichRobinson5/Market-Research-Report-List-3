<p><p>Film capacitors are widely used in electronic circuits for energy storage and signal filtering purposes. There are two main types: AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are designed to handle alternating current and are commonly used in applications such as motor-run and power factor correction. DC film capacitors, on the other hand, are specifically designed for direct current circuits. They are often found in power supplies, inverters, and DC link applications due to their high voltage and high energy storage capabilities. Both types play crucial roles in various electrical systems.</p></p>

<p><p>The electric modular grippers market consists of two main types: parallel grippers and angular grippers. Parallel grippers are designed with two opposing jaws that move in parallel to grip objects. They are versatile and commonly used in various industrial applications. On the other hand, angular grippers have jaws that move in an angular motion to grip objects from the sides. They are suitable for narrow spaces and provide better access to objects. Both types play a crucial role in automation processes, allowing efficient and precise handling of objects in different settings.</p></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>In-vehicle network communication market consists of two types: in-car wired connection and out-car wireless connection. In-car wired connection refers to the communication system within the vehicle that uses physical cables to connect various electronic components such as infotainment systems, sensors, and control units. This wired connection ensures reliable and high-speed data transmission. On the other hand, out-car wireless connection enables communication between the vehicle and external devices or networks, allowing features such as remote diagnostics, Over-The-Air (OTA) updates, and vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) communication. It eliminates the need for physical connections and provides flexibility and convenience.</p></p>

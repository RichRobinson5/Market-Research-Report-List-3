<p><p>Rail scale market includes static weighing and dynamic weighing. Static weighing involves weighing a stationary train using a rail scale system. This method ensures accurate measurement of the train's weight, allowing for proper loading and preventing overloaded trains. On the other hand, the dynamic weighing market involves weighing trains while they are in motion. This allows for continuous weight monitoring during train travel, ensuring compliance with weight regulations and preventing excessive wear on infrastructure. Both types of rail scales are essential for efficient and safe transportation of goods via railways.</p></p>

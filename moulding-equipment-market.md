<p><p>Moulding equipment market consists of various types such as vertical moulding equipment, flask moulding equipment, and match moulding equipment. Vertical moulding equipment is designed for producing vertical casting molds, while flask moulding equipment is used to create molds by packing sand around a pattern and then removing the pattern to leave the mold cavity. Match moulding equipment is specialized in making molds by igniting match-like sticks that create a desired shape. These different types of moulding equipment cater to specific production needs within the manufacturing industry.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Balanced Coil Systems</li><li>Ferrous-in-Foil Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical metal detector market consists of various types, including balanced coil systems and ferrous-in-foil systems. Balanced coil systems use two similar-sized coils positioned in opposite directions, producing a uniform field that detects metal contaminants in products passing through. Ferrous-in-foil systems are specialized metal detectors designed to detect ferrous metals, such as iron, that are concealed in a foil packaging. These systems provide pharmaceutical manufacturers with the ability to detect and remove metal contaminants, ensuring product safety and quality.</p></p>

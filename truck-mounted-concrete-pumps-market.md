<p><p>The truck mounted concrete pumps market consists of two main applications: line pumps and boom pumps. Boom pumps, on the other hand, utilize articulated robotic arms to deliver concrete with precision and flexibility, making them suitable for large construction projects. These applications cater to various construction needs, providing efficient and effective concrete placement solutions.</p></p>

<p><p>Pharma Track and Trace Solutions refers to the system used in the pharmaceutical industry to track and trace medication through the supply chain. Barcodes are a common market type used in this system, consisting of a series of black and white lines that represent a unique identification number for each product. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) is another market type used, where small electronic tags are attached to each product and can be scanned through radio frequency signals to capture data such as location and expiration dates. Both technologies aid in enhancing supply chain visibility and product authentication.</p></p>

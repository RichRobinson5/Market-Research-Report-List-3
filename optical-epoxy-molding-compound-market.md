<p><strong>The Optical Epoxy Molding Compound Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Optical EMC</li><li>Liquid Optical EMC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Epoxy Molding Compound (EMC) is available in two main types: Solid Optical EMC and Liquid Optical EMC. Solid Optical EMC is pre-cured and solidified before use, often in the form of pellets or sheets, making it easy to handle and store. On the other hand, Liquid Optical EMC is a liquid formulation that can be poured or injected into molds for shaping, offering more flexibility in production processes. Both types offer high optical clarity and are commonly used in the manufacturing of optical components such as lenses and prisms.</p></p>

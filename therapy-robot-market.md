<p><p>The therapy robot market can be divided into two main types: mobile therapy robots and stationary therapy robots. They are equipped with advanced mobility features that allow them to move around freely. On the other hand, stationary therapy robots are designed to stay in one place and provide therapy or companionship to individuals who may not have the capability to move around or interact with their surroundings. Both types of robots play a vital role in the field of therapy by offering companionship, emotional support, and assistance to patients.</p></p>

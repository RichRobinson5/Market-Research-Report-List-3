<p><p>Air gauges are measurement instruments that utilize air pressure to measure the dimensions of objects. The Air Ring Gauge is used to measure the external dimensions of cylindrical objects such as shafts, providing accurate readings. The Air Plug Gauge, on the other hand, measures the internal dimensions of objects, like holes or bores, ensuring precision. Other types of air gauges include air snap gauges and air jet gauges. These different gauges cater to specific measurement needs, allowing industries to maintain quality control and ensure proper fit and functioning of their products.</p></p>

<p><p>Medical suction regulators market can be classified into two main types: Continuous Suction Regulators and Intermittent Suction Regulators. Continuous Suction Regulators provide a steady and constant suction pressure for continuous drainage of bodily fluids during medical procedures. On the other hand, Intermittent Suction Regulators allow for periodic interruptions in suction pressure to prevent tissue damage and maintain optimal suction levels. Both types of regulators play a crucial role in medical settings for effective and safe removal of fluids from the body.</p></p>

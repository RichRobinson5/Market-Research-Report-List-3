<p><p>The explosion-proof platform scale market consists of two main types: integrated and distributed. Integrated platform scales refer to those that are designed to be used as a standalone weighing system, usually equipped with load cells and a display/control unit within the scale itself. On the other hand, distributed platform scales are part of a larger system where the weighing components are distributed throughout different locations, interconnected by a network. This allows for centralized control and monitoring, making it ideal for industrial settings where multiple weighing points are required.</p></p>

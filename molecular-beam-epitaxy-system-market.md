<p><ul><li>Normal MBE</li><li>Laser MBE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) System Market consists of two types: Normal MBE and Laser MBE. Normal MBE is a traditional method that uses thermal effusion cells to deposit materials onto a substrate, enabling high-quality layer growth. Laser MBE, on the other hand, utilizes laser ablation techniques to evaporate materials and achieve better control over growth rate and composition. Both systems are essential for fabricating advanced materials used in various industries like semiconductor, optoelectronics, and photovoltaics.</p></p>

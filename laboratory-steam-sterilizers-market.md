<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Laboratory Steam Sterilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Cycle</li><li>SFPP Cycle</li><li>Pre-Vac Cycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laboratory Steam Sterilizers market consists of three main types: Gravity Cycle, SFPP Cycle, and Pre-Vac Cycle. The Gravity Cycle utilizes gravity to remove air from the chamber, allowing steam to penetrate and sterilize the items inside. SFPP Cycle, or Steam Flush Pressure Pulse, involves multiple stages of steam flushing and pressure pulsing to ensure effective sterilization. Pre-Vac Cycle involves multiple vacuum and steam pulses to eliminate air and sterilize the chamber efficiently. These different types offer various methods to achieve sterilization in laboratory settings.</p></p>

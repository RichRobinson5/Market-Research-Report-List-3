<p><p>The Industrial Electric Screwdrivers market consists of two different types: Cordless Electric Screwdrivers and Corded Electric Screwdrivers. Cordless Electric Screwdrivers are powered by rechargeable batteries, allowing for more mobility and flexibility. They are commonly used in situations where portability is important. On the other hand, Corded Electric Screwdrivers need to be plugged into an electrical outlet for power. They provide a constant source of power, making them ideal for heavy-duty and continuous use. Both types serve the same purpose of efficiently driving screws in industrial applications, but differ in terms of power source and portability.</p></p>

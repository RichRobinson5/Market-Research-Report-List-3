<p><p>Fiberglass storage boxes are categorized into different market types based on the type of glass fibres used in their manufacturing. E-glass, C-glass, and A-glass are the most common types. E-glass refers to the ones made with electrical-grade glass fibers, known for their excellent electrical insulation properties. C-glass is manufactured with corrosion-resistant glass fibers, making it suitable for storage boxes used in harsh environments. A-glass is made with alkali-resistant glass fibers, making it ideal for storage boxes used in alkaline conditions. Additionally, there are other market types that may include unique variations or blends of glass fibers.</p></p>

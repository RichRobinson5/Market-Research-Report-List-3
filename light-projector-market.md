<p><p>The light projector market consists of various types, such as fixed-installation projectors and pico projectors. Fixed-installation projectors are designed for permanent installations in places like theaters, auditoriums, or conference rooms. They offer high brightness, image quality, and durability. On the other hand, pico projectors are small, portable devices mainly used for personal or on-the-go entertainment. They are compact in size and suitable for displaying content from smartphones, tablets, or laptops, making them ideal for small gatherings, outdoor events, or impromptu presentations.</p></p>

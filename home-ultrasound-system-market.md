<p><p>The home ultrasound system market can be categorized into two main types: wired ultrasound systems and wireless ultrasound systems. Wired ultrasound systems require a physical connection between the ultrasound probe and the imaging device, typically using cables. On the other hand, wireless ultrasound systems use advanced technology to establish a connection between the probe and the imaging device without the need for cables. Both types of systems offer the convenience of conducting ultrasound scans at home, but differ in terms of connectivity.</p></p>

<p><p>There are various types of iPSC (induced pluripotent stem cell) kits available in the market. These include adenovirus, Sendai virus, PiggyBac system, minicircle vector, episomal vector, and others. Adenovirus and Sendai virus kits are commonly used for iPSC reprogramming as they efficiently introduce necessary genes into cells. PiggyBac system offers stable transgene integration, while minicircle vector ensures reduced risk of gene disruption. Episomal vector kits enable non-integrating iPSC generation. Other kits may involve unique methods or vectors for iPSC reprogramming, expanding the options available to researchers and facilitating their specific needs.</p></p>

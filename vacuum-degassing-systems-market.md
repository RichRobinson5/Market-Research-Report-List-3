<p><p>Vacuum degassing systems are specialized equipment used to remove dissolved gases, such as oxygen and nitrogen, from liquids. These systems operate by creating a vacuum or low-pressure environment inside a vessel that contains the liquid, allowing the gases to escape through evaporation. Vacuum degassing is commonly used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, semiconductors, and water treatment, where the presence of dissolved gases can have adverse effects on product quality and performance.</p><p>The vacuum degassing systems market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.

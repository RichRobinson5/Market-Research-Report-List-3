<p><ul><li>Active Sonobuoy</li><li>Passive Sonobuoy</li><li>Special Purpose Sonobuoy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sonobuoys are underwater acoustic sensing devices used for military and commercial purposes. The active sonobuoy emits sound waves and listens for their reflections to detect underwater targets. Passive sonobuoys rely on listening to the sounds in their surroundings to detect and locate submarines or other vessels. Special purpose sonobuoys are designed for specific applications like environmental monitoring or search and rescue missions. The market for sonobuoys consists of these three types, each catering to different needs and requirements in underwater surveillance and detection.</p></p>

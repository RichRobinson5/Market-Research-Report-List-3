<p><p>Exhaust gas purifiers are devices used to reduce harmful pollutants emitted from vehicle exhaust systems. General Filter Type purifiers use filters to trap particulate matter before releasing the purified gas. Initiative Regeneration Type purifiers proactively burn particulate matter to keep the filter clean. Passive Regeneration Type purifiers rely on the high temperatures of the exhaust system to burn off the particulate matter. Fuel Injection Type purifiers inject a special fuel into the exhaust system for combustion. Microwave Regeneration Type purifiers use microwave energy to burn off particulate matter.</p></p>

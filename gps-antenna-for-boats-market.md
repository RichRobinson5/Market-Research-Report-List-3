<p><p>The GPS antenna for boats market consists of three main types: Normal Frequency, High Frequency, and Ultra Frequency. Normal Frequency antennas operate in the L1 band, providing accurate positioning data for navigation purposes. High Frequency antennas operate in the L1 and L2 bands, offering better accuracy and performance, particularly in challenging environments. Ultra Frequency antennas operate in the L1, L2, and L5 bands, providing the highest level of accuracy and precision for demanding applications such as hydrographic surveys and precision farming. Each market segment caters to specific user requirements and budget considerations.</p></p>

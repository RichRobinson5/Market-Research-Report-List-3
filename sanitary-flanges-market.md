<p><p>Sanitary flanges are used to connect pipes, valves, and other equipment in sanitary systems to ensure leak-proof connections and easy disassembly for cleaning and maintenance. There are various types of sanitary flanges available in the market. Slip-on flanges slide onto the pipe and are then welded. Blind flanges are used to close off the end of a pipe or valve. Long neck flanges have an extended neck that provides additional support and space for insulation or heating elements. Each type serves specific purposes in sanitary systems.</p></p>

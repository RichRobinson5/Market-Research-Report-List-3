<p><p>The yacht cranes market consists of different types of cranes used for lifting and maneuvering loads on yachts. Electric yacht cranes use electric power and are suitable for medium-sized loads. Hybrid yacht cranes combine hydraulic and electric systems, offering a balance between power and efficiency. These different types cater to the varying needs and requirements of yacht owners and operators in terms of load capacity and operation.</p></p>

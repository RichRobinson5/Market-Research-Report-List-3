<p><p>Solar street lighting systems can be classified into two main types: standalone and grid connected. Standalone solar street lighting systems are independent and operate autonomously using solar panels, batteries, and LED lights. These systems are ideal for remote areas with limited access to power sources. On the other hand, grid connected solar street lighting systems are connected to the main power grid and draw electricity when necessary. These systems are commonly used in urban areas where access to the power grid is readily available.</p></p>

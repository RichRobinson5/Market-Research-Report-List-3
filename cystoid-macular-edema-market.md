<p><p>Cystoid Macular Edema (CME) is a condition characterized by the accumulation of fluid in the macula, the central region of the retina responsible for detailed central vision. It occurs due to the inflammation, damage, or blockage of blood vessels in the eye, leading to the leakage of fluid into the macula. CME can result from various underlying causes, such as age-related macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, uveitis, or post cataract surgery. The condition can cause blurred or distorted vision, decreased visual acuity, and central scotomas.</p><p>The Cystoid Macular Edema Market is anticipated to exhibit steady growth during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the rising prevalence of diseases that can lead to CME, such as diabetes and age-related macular degeneration.

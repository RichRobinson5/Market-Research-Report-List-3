<p><p>Pump feeders are a type of machinery used in various industries to transport and dispense materials. There are two main types of pump feeders: volumetric pump feeders and gravitational pump feeders. </p><p>Volumetric pump feeders operate by measuring a specific volume of material and then dispensing it. This ensures accurate and consistent delivery of materials.</p><p>On the other hand, gravitational pump feeders rely on the force of gravity to move materials through a system. This type of feeder is commonly used when materials are free-flowing and do not require precise measurements.</p><p>Both types of pump feeders play crucial roles in different industrial processes, offering efficient and reliable material handling solutions.</p></p>

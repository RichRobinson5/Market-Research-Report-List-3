<p><strong>The Rescue Helmets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABS Rescue Helmets</li><li>HDPE Rescue Helmets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rescue Helmets market consists of two main types: ABS Rescue Helmets and HDPE Rescue Helmets. ABS (acrylonitrile butadiene styrene) helmets are made of a durable thermoplastic material that provides excellent impact resistance and protection. They are widely used in firefighting and other emergency response situations. On the other hand, HDPE (high-density polyethylene) helmets are lighter in weight and offer good electrical insulation properties. They are commonly used in industrial settings where there is a risk of electrical hazards. Both types of helmets are essential safety equipment for professionals involved in rescue operations.</p></p>

<p><p>Synthetic casting tape refers to a type of material used in orthopedic applications for immobilizing and supporting fractures or injured limbs. Fibreglass casting tape is made of woven fibreglass with a resin that hardens when exposed to moisture, providing rigid and lightweight support. Polyester casting tape, on the other hand, is made of woven polyester fibers with a water-activated resin, which creates a strong and durable cast. Both types of casting tapes offer similar benefits, such as ease of application, breathability, and radiolucency, while providing different options based on individual needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The outdoor humidity sensors market consists of two main types: wired outdoor humidity sensors and wireless outdoor humidity sensors. Wireless sensors transmit data through wireless protocols such as Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, enabling remote monitoring and data collection.</p></p>

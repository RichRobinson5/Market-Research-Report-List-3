<p><ul><li>T-Shank</li><li>U-Shank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jigsaw blades are categorized into two types based on the shank design, T-Shank and U-Shank. T-Shank blades have a straight shank that is inserted and secured into the jigsaw, forming a T-shape. These blades are widely used and easily interchangeable across different jigsaw models. On the other hand, U-Shank blades have a U-shaped shank that requires a screw to secure them in place. This type of blade is less common but still used in certain jigsaw models. Both types serve the purpose of cutting various materials with precision and efficiency.</p></p>

<p><p>The Distance Measurement Sensor market comprises various types of sensors. Infra-Red LED sensors use infrared light to measure distance. Ultrasonic sensors emit and receive sound waves to measure distance. Laser diode sensors utilize laser beams for distance measurement. Photoelectric sensors measure distance using the reflection of light. Draw wire sensors estimate distance through the extension of a wire. Image sensors capture distance based on image analysis. "Others" refer to different types of sensors used for distance measurement in various industries.</p></p>

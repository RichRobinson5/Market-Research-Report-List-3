<p><p>Blockchain in logistics market consists of various types such as public blockchain, private blockchain, and others. In a public blockchain, anyone can participate, view, and validate transactions, providing transparency but compromising privacy. On the other hand, private blockchain limits participation to a select group, ensuring confidentiality but reducing transparency. Other market types include consortium blockchain where a group of organizations maintains the network collectively, and hybrid blockchain which combines characteristics of both public and private blockchains. These various market types cater to different needs and preferences in terms of security, control, and accessibility in the logistics industry.</p></p>

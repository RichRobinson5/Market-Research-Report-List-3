<p><p>Motorcycle disc brakes are available in two main types - mechanical disc brakes and hydraulic disc brakes. Mechanical disc brakes operate using cables to actuate the brake calipers, offering a simple and cost-effective braking solution. On the other hand, hydraulic disc brakes use fluid pressure to transfer force to the calipers, providing more precise and powerful braking performance. Both types have their own set of advantages and are used in various motorcycles based on the rider's preference and requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>The laser drilling machine for aerospace market consists of three main types: YAG laser drilling machine, fiber laser drilling machine, and CO2 laser drilling machine. YAG laser drilling machines use crystal rods to generate laser beams, which are then focused through optics. Fiber laser drilling machines utilize fiber optic cables to transmit laser beams for drilling purposes. CO2 laser drilling machines, on the other hand, use carbon dioxide gas as the laser medium. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the aerospace industry.</p></p>

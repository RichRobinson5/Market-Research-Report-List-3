<p><p>CW (Continuous Wave) radar systems are used for various applications including civilian, military, and automotive purposes. The market is segmented based on the operating frequency bands, including L Band, X Band, C Band, and S Band. L Band radar operates within the 1-2 GHz frequency range and is utilized for long range surveillance and navigation applications. X Band radar operates between 8-12 GHz and is known for its high resolution and accurate tracking capabilities. C Band radar operates at 4-8 GHz and is commonly used for weather and air traffic control. S Band radar operates between 2-4 GHz and is often employed for short-range air surveillance and weather forecasting purposes.</p></p>

<p><p>AOP solutions are used in various markets like photochemical, catalytic wet, sonochemical, ozone, electrochemical, and Fenton. Photochemical AOP involves using light to initiate chemical reactions, while catalytic wet AOP utilizes catalysts in a wet environment. Sonochemical AOP uses sound waves to enhance reactions, ozone AOP utilizes ozone gas, electrochemical AOP involves using electricity, and Fenton AOP includes the use of iron catalysts. Each market type offers unique benefits and applications for treating various contaminants in water and wastewater treatment processes.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Wireless Health Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WLAN/Wi-Fi</li><li>WPAN</li><li>WiMAX</li><li>WWAN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless health market can be categorized into four market types. Firstly, WLAN/Wi-Fi refers to Wireless Local Area Network, which enables communication within a limited area using radio waves. Secondly, WPAN stands for Wireless Personal Area Network, providing short-range wireless connectivity for personal devices. Thirdly, WiMAX, or Worldwide Interoperability for Microwave Access, offers long-range wireless communication for broader coverage areas. Lastly, WWAN represents Wireless Wide Area Network, enabling wireless connectivity over large geographic regions. These market types serve different purposes in the wireless health industry, catering to various communication needs within healthcare settings.</p></p>

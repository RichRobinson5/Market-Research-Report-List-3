<p><p>The disinfectant equipment market consists of two main types: chemical methods of disinfection and physical methods of disinfection. Chemical methods involve the use of disinfectants, such as chlorine, hydrogen peroxide, or alcohol, to kill or inactivate microorganisms on surfaces or in the air. Physical methods, on the other hand, utilize non-chemical means to achieve disinfection, such as heat (steam or hot water), ultraviolet (UV) radiation, or filtration. Both these methods play a crucial role in preventing the spread of infections and maintaining cleanliness in various industries, including healthcare, food and beverages, and manufacturing.</p></p>

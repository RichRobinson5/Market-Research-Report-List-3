<p><p>Modular Instruments refer to a type of electronic test and measurement equipment that can be configured and customized based on specific user requirements. The PXI, VXI, and AXIe are different types of modular instrument markets. PXI (PCI eXtensions for Instrumentation) is based on the PCI bus and is known for its high-speed data transfer capability. VXI (VME eXtensions for Instrumentation) is based on the VME bus and is renowned for its robustness and versatility. AXIe (AdvancedTCA eXtensions for Instrumentation) utilizes the AdvancedTCA architecture and offers increased bandwidth and performance for large systems with advanced features.</p></p>

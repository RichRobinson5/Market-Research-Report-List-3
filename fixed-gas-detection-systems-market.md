<p><p>Fixed Gas Detection Systems are used to continuously monitor and detect the presence of toxic and combustible gases in industrial settings to ensure the safety of personnel and equipment. There are two main types of market systems: Wired Gas Detection Systems and Wireless Gas Detection Systems. </p><p>Wired Gas Detection Systems use a physical connection to transmit signals between the gas sensors and the control panel. This type of system is reliable and provides real-time data, but installation can be time-consuming and costly.</p><p>Wireless Gas Detection Systems, on the other hand, use wireless communication to transmit signals. This makes installation easier and more flexible, allowing for easy expansion and relocation of sensors. However, it may not be as reliable as wired systems due to potential signal interference or loss.</p></p>

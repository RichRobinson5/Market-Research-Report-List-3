<p><p>Abdominal Aortic Covered Stents market includes two main types: Bifurcated Type and Straight Tube Type. The Bifurcated Type stents are designed with branches to accommodate the branching of the aorta, providing an effective seal and support for the vessel. On the other hand, Straight Tube Type stents are designed for a single straight segment of the aorta, providing a seamless cover and reinforcement for the damaged area. Both types of stents offer different solutions for treating abdominal aortic aneurysms and related conditions.</p></p>

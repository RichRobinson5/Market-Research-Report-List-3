<p><p>Universal changeover conversion plug market can be divided into two types based on pressure: variable pressure and invariable pressure. Variable pressure plugs are designed to automatically adjust to different voltage levels, providing flexibility in various countries with fluctuating power supplies. Invariable pressure plugs, on the other hand, are set to a specific voltage level and do not change regardless of the input voltage. Both types cater to the needs of travelers and businesses alike, offering a convenient solution for powering devices in different regions.</p></p>

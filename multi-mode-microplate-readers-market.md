<p><strong>The Multi-mode Microplate Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filter-based Readers</li><li>Monochromators-based Reader</li><li>Hybrid Readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-mode microplate readers are versatile instruments capable of performing various types of assays. Filter-based readers use different filters to isolate specific wavelengths of light for each assay. Monochromators-based readers utilize a grating system to select a range of wavelengths, providing flexibility for different assays. Hybrid readers combine the benefits of both filter-based and monochromators-based systems, offering a wide range of detection options for researchers. Each type of reader has its own advantages and is selected based on the specific requirements of the assay being performed.</p></p>

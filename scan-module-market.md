<p><p>The scan module market can be categorized into three types: 1D scan module, 2D scan module, and others. The 1D scan module is designed to scan one-dimensional barcodes, while the 2D scan module can read two-dimensional barcodes, including QR codes and PDF417 codes. The "others" category includes scan modules with specific functionalities that are not covered by the previous two types, such as modules with RFID and NFC capabilities. These modules are used in various industries, including retail, logistics, healthcare, and transportation, to improve efficiency and streamline operations.</p></p>

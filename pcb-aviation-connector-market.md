<p><p>The PCB Aviation Connector market comprises two main types: Circular Type and Rectangular Type connectors. Circular Type connectors are round-shaped and are typically used in applications that require secure and durable connections, such as aviation and military industries. They offer high resistance to vibration, moisture, and dust. Rectangular Type connectors have a rectangular shape and are commonly used in applications requiring space-saving and high-density connections, like telecommunication and computing industries. They provide easy installation and versatility in terms of pin configuration, offering reliable performance in various environments.</p></p>

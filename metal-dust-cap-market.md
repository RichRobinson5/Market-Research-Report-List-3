<p><p>A metal dust cap, also known as a wheel dust cap or wheel center cap, is a protective cover that is used to seal the center of a wheel hub. Metal dust caps are commonly used in automotive, industrial, and aviation applications.</p><p>The metal dust cap market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.

<p><p>Advanced Power Modules for Industrial Market Types consist of two main categories: Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) and Power Integrated Modules (PIMs). IPMs combine power devices, driver circuits, and protection mechanisms in a single package, offering enhanced efficiency, reliability, and safety. PIMs are similar in function to IPMs but generally offer a lower level of integration. Both types of modules are widely used in industrial applications such as motor drives, inverters, and power supplies to improve system performance and simplify design.</p></p>

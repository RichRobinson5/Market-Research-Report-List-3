<p><p>Sanding pads are abrasive materials used for smoothing surfaces. The market for sanding pads can be categorized into various types, including discs, wheels, rolls, and others. Discs are flat-shaped pads commonly used for sanding flat surfaces. Wheels are circular pads that can be attached to power tools for efficient sanding of curved or irregular surfaces. Rolls are long strips of sanding material used for hand sanding or for wrapping around cylindrical objects. The "others" category includes specialty pads designed for specific applications, such as detail sanding or sanding in tight corners.</p></p>

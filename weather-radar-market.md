<p><p>Weather radar is a type of radar system used to detect and track precipitation, as well as other atmospheric phenomena such as thunderstorms, tornadoes, and hail. <p><p>The Weather Radar market can be divided into two main types: Airborne Weather Radar and Land-based Weather Radar. Airborne Weather Radars are installed on aircraft and are used to gather data on weather conditions while in flight. They provide information on precipitation, turbulence, and other weather patterns to aid in navigation and flight safety. On the other hand, Land-based Weather Radars are fixed installations on the ground that analyze atmospheric conditions, providing weather forecasts and warnings for specific regions. Both types play crucial roles in monitoring and predicting weather conditions for various applications.</p></p>

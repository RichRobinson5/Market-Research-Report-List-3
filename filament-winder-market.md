<p><p>A filament winder is a machine used for the automated production of composite products such as pipes, tubing, tanks, and other cylindrical structures. It consists of a rotating mandrel, a delivery system for the filament material (usually fiberglass or carbon fiber), and a machine head that moves along the length of the mandrel, depositing the filament and creating the desired shape.</p><p>The filament winder market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for lightweight and high-strength composite materials in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction. This trend is expected to continue as companies strive to improve efficiency and reduce labor costs.</p><p>Another emerging trend in the filament winder market is the use of advanced materials in composite production.

<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner-rotor Motor</li><li>External-rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel motor market consists of two main types: Inner-rotor motors and External-rotor motors. Inner-rotor motors have the rotor located inside the stator, providing a more compact design with higher torque density. On the other hand, External-rotor motors have the rotor located on the outside of the stator, offering better cooling capabilities and efficiency. Both types have their advantages and are used in electric vehicles for different applications based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>

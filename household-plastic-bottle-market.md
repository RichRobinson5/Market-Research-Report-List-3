<p><p>The household plastic bottle market is divided into various types, including PP (polypropylene), PE (polyethylene), ABS (acrylonitrile butadiene styrene), PET (polyethylene terephthalate), and Others. PP bottles are known for their durability and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for storing cleaning agents. PE bottles are flexible and commonly used for packaging liquids. ABS bottles offer excellent impact resistance and are often used for packaging cosmetic and personal care products. PET bottles are transparent and widely used for beverages. The "Others" category includes bottles made from different materials like HDPE (high-density polyethylene) and PVC (polyvinyl chloride).</p></p>

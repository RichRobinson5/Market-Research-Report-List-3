<p><p>Reconfigurable Optical Add/Drop Multiplexing (ROADM) is a technology used in optical networks to dynamically add, drop, or reroute wavelengths of light signals. The market includes various components. Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS) enables selective routing of different wavelengths. Optical Channel Monitoring (OCM) allows real-time monitoring of optical signals for performance optimization. Variable Optical Attenuators (VOAs) help control the strength of optical signals. These components play crucial roles in optimizing the efficiency and flexibility of optical networks, allowing for more seamless and efficient data transmission.</p></p>

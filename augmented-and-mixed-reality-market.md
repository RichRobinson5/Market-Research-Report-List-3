<p><p>Augmented Reality (AR) is a market type that includes Head Mounted Displays (HMDs) and Head Up Displays (HUDs). HMDs are worn on the head and immerse the user in a completely virtual environment. They overlay digital information onto the real world, enhancing the user's perception. HUDs, on the other hand, display digital content directly in the user's line of sight without completely obstructing their view. They are typically used in vehicles to provide information like speed and navigation. Both AR technologies offer a seamless integration of digital and real-world content for various applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Night vision goggles are devices used to enhance visibility in low-light or dark conditions. There are two main types of night vision goggles: image intensifier and thermal image goggles. Image intensifier goggles work by amplifying the existing ambient light, making objects visible to the human eye. On the other hand, thermal image goggles detect heat signatures emitted by objects, converting them into images. While image intensifier goggles are widely used by law enforcement and military personnel, thermal image goggles are primarily used for surveillance or detecting heat sources in a variety of applications.</p></p>

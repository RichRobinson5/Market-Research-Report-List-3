Compression molding involves placing a heated plastic material into a mold and applying pressure to shape it. Injection molding entails injecting melted plastic into a mold under high pressure. Turning molding is achieved by spinning a plastic material against a mold to shape it. These manufacturing techniques play a crucial role in producing plastic aspheric lenses, catering to the diverse needs of industries like optics, electronics, and healthcare.</p></p>

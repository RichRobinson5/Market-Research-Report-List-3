<p><p>Full-Managed Outsourcing in the medical device manufacturing industry refers to a situation where the entire manufacturing process is outsourced to a third-party manufacturer. This means that the outsourcing company relinquishes control of the manufacturing process entirely to the manufacturer. </p><p>Co-Managed Outsourcing, on the other hand, involves a collaborative arrangement between the outsourcing company and the manufacturer. The outsourcing company retains some level of control over the manufacturing process, while the manufacturer takes on the primary responsibility for production. It is a partnership where both parties work together to ensure a successful manufacturing operation.</p></p>

On the other hand, welding type expansion joints are specifically welded to the pipeline, eliminating the need for flanges. Both types serve the purpose of absorbing thermal expansion, reducing vibration, and preventing leaks in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and power generation.</p></p>

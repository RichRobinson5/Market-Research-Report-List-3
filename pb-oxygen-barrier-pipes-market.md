<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PB Oxygen Barrier Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H)</li><li>Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PB Oxygen Barrier Pipes market can be categorized into two types based on the type of polybutene-1 used in their production. Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) pipes are made from pure polybutene-1, offering excellent oxygen barrier properties to prevent corrosion in heating systems. On the other hand, Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R) pipes are produced using a mix of polybutene-1 and ethylene, providing a balance of flexibility and strength with effective oxygen barrier capabilities. Both types cater to the specific needs of different applications in the market.</p></p>

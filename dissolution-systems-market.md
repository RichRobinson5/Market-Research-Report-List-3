<p><p>Dissolution systems are used in the pharmaceutical industry to test the dissolution rate of drug formulations. There are two types of dissolution systems: offline and online. The offline system involves removing samples at predetermined time intervals and analyzing them separately. This method is time-consuming and requires manual intervention. On the other hand, the online system continuously monitors and analyzes the drug dissolution in real-time, providing automated and accurate results. Online systems offer faster testing, reduced human error, and improved efficiency compared to offline systems.</p></p>

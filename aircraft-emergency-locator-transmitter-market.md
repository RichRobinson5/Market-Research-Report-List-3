<p><p>The Aircraft Emergency Locator Transmitter (ELT) market consists of two main types: embedded and portable ELTs. Embedded ELTs are permanently installed in aircraft and are activated automatically in the event of an accident or emergency, transmitting distress signals to search and rescue teams. Portable ELTs, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be easily carried on board an aircraft and manually activated when needed. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of aircraft and passengers by enabling rapid and accurate location during emergencies.</p></p>

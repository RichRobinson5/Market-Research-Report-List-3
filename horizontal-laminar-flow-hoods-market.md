They come in two types: HEPA filter and ULPA filter. HEPA filters effectively remove 99.97% of particles of size 0.3 microns or larger, while ULPA filters offer even higher efficiency by removing particles as small as 0.12 microns with a 99.9995% effectiveness. Both filters provide clean air by pushing it through the filter in a horizontal direction, ensuring a dust-free and sterile environment for sensitive processes.</p></p>

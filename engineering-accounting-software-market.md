<p><p>Engineering accounting software market types can be categorized as cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage their accounting data through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and ease of use. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed and operated on the user's local computer systems. It provides more control and security as data is stored within the organization's infrastructure. Both types have their advantages and suitability based on the organization's requirements, infrastructure, and the level of control and accessibility needed.</p></p>

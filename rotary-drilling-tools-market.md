<p><p>Rotary drilling tools are indispensable equipment in the construction industry and are available in various types. Augers are spiral-shaped tools used for drilling holes in the ground. Drilling buckets are cylindrical containers with teeth that help excavate soil. Core barrels are used to extract cylindrical samples of rocks and soil. Temporary casing is a tool used to support the walls of a borehole during drilling operations. These tools play a crucial role in the efficient and precise execution of drilling projects.</p></p>

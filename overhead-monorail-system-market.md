<p><p>An overhead monorail system is a type of transportation system that uses a single rail track located above the ground to transport goods or people. It typically consists of a series of suspended carriages or a single carriage that moves along the track, supported and guided by the overhead rail. Overhead monorails are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, warehousing, and material handling to efficiently move heavy loads within a facility.</p><p>In terms of market growth analysis, the global overhead monorail system market is predicted to experience substantial growth in the coming years.

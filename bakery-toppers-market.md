<p><p>The bakery toppers market can be classified into two main types: edible and non-edible toppers. Edible toppers are made from food-grade ingredients and are intended to be consumed along with the bakery item. They enhance the taste and visual appeal of the product. Non-edible toppers, on the other hand, are typically made from materials like plastic or paper. They are used for decoration purposes only and should not be consumed.

<p><p>Blood pressure monitors are devices used to measure the force of blood against artery walls. There are three main types of blood pressure monitors in the market. Upper-arm blood pressure monitors are the most common and accurate ones, involving a cuff wrapped around the upper arm to measure blood pressure. Wrist blood pressure monitors provide a convenient alternative, as they are placed on the wrist and inflated to measure blood pressure. Lastly, there are other types of blood pressure monitors available, but they are less commonly used and may include finger monitors or smartphone apps.</p></p>

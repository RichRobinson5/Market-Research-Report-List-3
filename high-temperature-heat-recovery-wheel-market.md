<p><strong>The High Temperature Heat Recovery Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensation Rotors</li><li>Hygroscopic Rotors</li><li>Sorption Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high temperature heat recovery wheel market consists of three main types of heat recovery wheels - condensation rotors, hygroscopic rotors, and sorption rotors. Condensation rotors use the condensation of moisture to recover heat from exhaust air. Hygroscopic rotors use the moisture absorption and desorption process to recover heat. Sorption rotors employ a combination of adsorption and desorption of moisture to recover heat. These technologies are commonly used in HVAC systems and industrial processes to improve energy efficiency and reduce operational costs.</p></p>

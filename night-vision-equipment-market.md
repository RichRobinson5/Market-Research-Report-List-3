<p><strong>The Night Vision Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Night vision equipment refers to devices that enable users to see in low-light or dark conditions. There are two main types of night vision equipment: image intensifiers and thermal image devices.</p><p>Image intensifiers work by collecting minimal available light and amplifying it to produce a brighter image. They rely on technologies like photomultiplier tubes or micro-channel plates to enhance the light.</p><p>Thermal image devices, on the other hand, detect heat emitted by objects and convert it into a visible image. These devices are helpful in detecting temperature differences and identifying objects or living beings based on their heat signatures.</p><p>Both image intensifiers and thermal image equipment serve different purposes in various industries, such as military, law enforcement, surveillance, and outdoor recreation.</p></p>

<p><p>A Cardan joint coupling, also known as a universal joint, is a mechanical device used to connect shafts that are positioned at different angles to each other. Its design enables torque transmission even when there is misalignment between the shafts. The coupling consists of a cross-shaped component with four bearing caps that house needle roller bearings. These bearings allow for the freedom of movement required for the shafts to rotate smoothly.</p><p>The Cardan joint coupling market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors.

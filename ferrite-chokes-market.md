<p><p>Ferrite chokes are commonly used in electronic devices to suppress high-frequency noise and interference. The market for ferrite chokes can be classified into two types: chip ferrite chokes and through-hole ferrite chokes. Chip ferrite chokes are small, surface-mount components widely used in compact electronic devices. On the other hand, through-hole ferrite chokes are larger components with wire leads that are soldered onto a circuit board. Both types serve the same purpose of filtering out unwanted signals, but differ in size and installation method.</p></p>

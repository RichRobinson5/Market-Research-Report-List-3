<p><strong>The Automotive Side Window Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tempered</li><li>Laminated</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive side window glass market is segmented into three main types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated to increase strength and shatter resistance, making it a popular choice for side windows. Laminated glass consists of multiple layers with a polymer interlayer that holds the glass together upon impact, providing increased safety and sound insulation. The "others" category includes specialized glass types like acoustic glass or self-dimming glass that offer unique features for automotive applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The Hidden Camera Detection Devices market includes two types: RF (Radio Frequency) Based and LED Light Gauge Based devices. RF Based devices use electromagnetic waves to detect hidden cameras by scanning for signals emitted by these devices. LED Light Gauge Based devices utilize laser technology to detect hidden cameras by illuminating the area and analyzing the reflections off camera lenses. Both types of devices are designed to identify and locate hidden cameras covertly and help maintain privacy and security in various settings.</p></p>

Double lumen catheters provide two separate channels, enabling simultaneous drug infusion and blood withdrawal. Multiple lumen catheters offer three or more channels, allowing for various medical procedures to be conducted simultaneously. These variations cater to the diverse needs of healthcare professionals, offering flexibility and convenience during vascular access procedures.</p></p>

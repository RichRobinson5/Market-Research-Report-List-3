<p><ul><li>Straight Guidewire</li><li>Elbow Guidewire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrophilic coating guidewires are available in two main types: straight guidewires and elbow guidewires. Straight guidewires have a uniform, straight design for easy insertion and navigation through blood vessels. Elbow guidewires, on the other hand, have a curved or bent tip that allows for precise steering around corners and through complex vascular structures. Both types of guidewires are commonly used in minimally invasive procedures to guide medical devices to specific locations within the body with greater ease and precision.</p></p>

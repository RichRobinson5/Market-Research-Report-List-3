<p><p>The Robot Window Cleaner market includes three types: Vacuum Suction, Fan Adsorption, and Brushless Motor Adsorption. Vacuum Suction uses a powerful suction system to attach the robot to the window, ensuring secure cleaning. Fan Adsorption, on the other hand, utilizes a fan to create suction, enabling the robot to adhere to the window surface. Lastly, Brushless Motor Adsorption employs a brushless motor to generate vacuum pressure for attaching the robot to the window. These market types offer various options to suit different user preferences and ensure efficient and effective window cleaning.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Metal Beverage Packaging Can Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Pieces Can</li><li>Three-Pieces Can</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal beverage packaging can market can be classified into two main types: two-pieces can and three-pieces can. </p><p>A two-pieces can is constructed from two components, namely, the body and the end. The body is formed by shaping a single piece of metal into a tubular structure, while the end is a separate metal component added to seal the can. This type of can is most commonly used for beverages like beer and soda.</p><p>On the other hand, a three-pieces can consists of three separate components - the body, end, and a separate bottom. Unlike the two-pieces can, the bottom is not a part of the body. This type of can is typically used for high-pressure beverages like carbonated drinks, as it provides better structural integrity.</p></p>

<p><p>The natural dissolvable sutures market offers different types of sutures, including Catgut Plain Sutures and Catgut Chromic Sutures. Catgut Plain Sutures are made from the submucosal layer of sheep intestines, while Catgut Chromic Sutures are treated with a chromium salt solution to increase the absorption time. Both sutures are naturally derived and gradually dissolve in the body over time, eliminating the need for suture removal. These sutures are commonly used in surgeries, providing secure wound closure and reducing the risk of infection.</p></p>

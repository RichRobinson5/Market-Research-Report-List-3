<p><p>The OLED common layer materials market types mainly include Electron Injection Layer (EIL), Electron Transport Layer (ETL), Hole Injection Layer (HIL), and Electron Transport Layer (HTL). The Electron Injection Layer facilitates the injection of electrons into the OLED, while the Electron Transport Layer enhances the electron mobility within the device. The Hole Injection Layer enables the injection of positively charged particles, called holes, into the OLED, and the Electron Transport Layer further enhances their mobility for efficient device operation. These materials play crucial roles in ensuring the proper functioning of OLED displays and lighting devices.</p></p>

<p><p>A BP (Blood Pressure) cuff, also known as a sphygmomanometer, is a medical device used to measure blood pressure in patients. It consists of an inflatable cuff that is wrapped around the patient's arm, a pressure gauge, and a rubber bulb to inflate the cuff. The cuff is used to temporarily stop the blood flow in the arm, and the pressure gauge measures the force of the blood when it starts flowing again, providing an accurate reading of the patient's blood pressure.</p><p>The BP cuff market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the increasing prevalence of hypertension and cardiovascular diseases worldwide. Factors such as sedentary lifestyles, unhealthy dietary habits, and rising elderly population have contributed to the rising demand for BP cuffs. Additionally, the growing awareness regarding the importance of regular blood pressure monitoring as a preventive measure has further boosted market growth.</p><p>Technological advancements have also played a crucial role in the market's growth. The introduction of digital BP cuffs that provide automated and more accurate readings has gained popularity among healthcare professionals and patients. Furthermore, the integration of wireless connectivity features and compatibility with smartphones has increased the convenience and accessibility of BP cuffs, driving market growth.</p><p>The COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the demand for BP cuffs as healthcare facilities have focused on monitoring patients' blood pressure as part of their overall assessment during the diagnosis and treatment of the virus.</p><p>In conclusion, the BP cuff market is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 9.60% during the forecast period. <p><strong>The BP Cuff Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-airbag BP Cuff</li><li>Multi-airbag BP Cuff</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The BP cuff market includes two types: single-airbag and multi-airbag BP cuffs. Single-airbag BP cuffs consist of a single inflatable chamber, which is wrapped around the upper arm to measure blood pressure. These cuffs are simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, multi-airbag BP cuffs have multiple inflatable chambers, allowing for a more accurate measurement of blood pressure by individually inflating and deflating each chamber. Although more complex and expensive, multi-airbag cuffs provide better accuracy and are commonly used in medical settings.</p></p>

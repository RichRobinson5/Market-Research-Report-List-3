<p><p>The air conditioning repair market can be categorized into two main types: whole-home and split systems. Whole-home air conditioning repair involves fixing and maintaining the central air conditioning system that cools the entire house. Split systems, on the other hand, refer to air conditioning units with indoor and outdoor components that are widely used in residential and commercial spaces. These systems require specialized repair services to fix issues such as refrigerant leaks, compressor failures, or sensor malfunctions to ensure the efficient cooling of individual rooms or areas.</p></p>

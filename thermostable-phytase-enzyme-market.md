<p><strong>The Thermostable Phytase Enzyme Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Histidine acid phosphatases (HAPs)</li><li>Β-propeller phytases</li><li>Purple acid phosphatases</li><li>Protein tyrosine phosphatase-like phytases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermostable phytase enzymes are classified into different types based on their structure and function. Histidine acid phosphatases (HAPs) are a type of enzyme that catalyze the hydrolysis of phytic acid. Β-propeller phytases are enzymes that specifically target phytate molecules. Purple acid phosphatases are a class of enzymes that can hydrolyze a variety of phosphate esters. Protein tyrosine phosphatase-like phytases are enzymes that cleave phosphate groups from tyrosine residues in proteins. Each type of thermostable phytase enzyme plays a specific role in the degradation of phytate in plant-based feed.</p></p>

<p><p>The expansion joint market includes various types such as axial expansion joints, angular expansion joints, lateral expansion joints, and universal expansion joints. Axial expansion joints are designed to accommodate axial movement, while angular expansion joints can handle angular movements. Lateral expansion joints are intended for lateral movements, and universal expansion joints are capable of accommodating multiple types of movements. Each type of expansion joint serves a specific purpose in ensuring the safe and reliable functioning of various industrial systems and structures that experience thermal or mechanical expansions.</p></p>

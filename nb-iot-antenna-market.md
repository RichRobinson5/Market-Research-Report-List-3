They are commonly used in small and portable IoT devices. On the other hand, external antennas are separate and detachable components. They offer flexibility in terms of placement and can be mounted externally, allowing for better signal reception. External antennas are typically deployed in larger IoT devices or in areas with weak network coverage.</p></p>

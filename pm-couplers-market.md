<p><p>PM (Polarization-Maintaining) couplers are essential components in fiber optic communication systems. They help manage and distribute optical signals effectively. The market for PM couplers can be classified into three types based on the number of ports: 1*2 ports, 2*2 ports, and Others. 1*2 port couplers have one input and two outputs, allowing a signal to split or combine. Similarly, 2*2 port couplers have two inputs and two outputs, enabling signal distribution or combining. The "Others" market refers to PM couplers with different configurations, such as specialized couplers or those with additional ports.</p></p>

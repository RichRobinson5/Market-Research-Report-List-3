<p><p>The market for textile fabric manufacturing machines comprises various types of machines. Circular knitting machines are used to produce seamless knitted fabrics. Warp knitting machines and stitch-bonding machines are used for making warp-knitted fabrics with different patterns and designs. Warping machines are used to create the warp yarn, which is the vertically aligned yarn used in weaving. Sizing machines apply a protective coating to the warp yarn to enhance its strength. Looms are used in the weaving process to interlace the warp and weft yarns, producing the final fabric.</p></p>

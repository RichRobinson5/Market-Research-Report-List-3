<p><p>Ball screw jacks are mechanical devices used for lifting, lowering, and positioning heavy loads. There are two main types: worm gear ball screw jacks and electro-mechanical screw jacks. Worm gear ball screw jacks utilize a worm gear drive system along with a ball screw, providing high efficiency and load transfer capability. On the other hand, electro-mechanical screw jacks use an electric motor to drive the screw, offering precise control and smooth operation. Both types find applications in various industries that require reliable and controlled lifting or positioning of heavy objects.</p></p>

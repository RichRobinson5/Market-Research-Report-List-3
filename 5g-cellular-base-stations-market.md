<p><p>5G cellular base stations are categorized based on their coverage area and deployment size. Macro base stations provide wide coverage and are usually installed on towers or tall buildings. Small base stations, also known as micro base stations, cover smaller areas and are installed on lampposts or rooftops. Pico base stations offer even smaller coverage and are often used indoors, such as in shopping malls. Lastly, femto base stations have the smallest coverage area and are mainly utilized in homes or small offices for personal use.</p></p>

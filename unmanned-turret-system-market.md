This allows for real-time control and monitoring of the turret system remotely. On the other hand, pre-programmed routines involve setting up predetermined instructions for the turret system to follow, ensuring automated and consistent operation without the need for constant human intervention. Both these market types contribute to the advancement of unmanned turret systems.</p></p>

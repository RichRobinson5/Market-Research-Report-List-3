<p><strong>The Magnetic Sheet Floaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet</li><li>Electromagnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnetic Sheet Floaters market is divided into two types: Permanent Magnet and Electromagnetic. </p><p>Permanent Magnet floaters utilize a fixed magnetism generated by materials like ferrite or neodymium, providing a consistent magnetic force to hold the sheets in place. These floaters require no external power source, making them cost-effective and easy to maintain.</p><p>Electromagnetic floaters, on the other hand, employ an electric current flowing through a coil to generate a temporary magnetic field. This adjustable magnetic force allows for flexible sheet manipulation and greater control over the floating process. However, electromagnetic floaters require an external power supply to function.</p></p>

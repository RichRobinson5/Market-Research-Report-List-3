<p><p>Log arch market types include Junior Arch and Buck Arch. Junior Arch log arch is a smaller, lightweight log arch designed for smaller logs or lighter loads. It is often used by homeowners or small-scale loggers. On the other hand, Buck Arch log arch is a larger, heavy-duty log arch suitable for larger logs or heavier loads. It is commonly used by professional loggers or in commercial logging operations. Both Junior Arch and Buck Arch serve the purpose of efficiently moving logs from one location to another, but they differ in terms of size and capacity.</p></p>

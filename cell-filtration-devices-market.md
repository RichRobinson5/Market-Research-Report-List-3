<p><p>Cell Filtration Devices market includes various types such as Bench Scale Vacuum-Driven Devices, Bench Scale Pressure-Driven Devices, Lab Scale Vacuum-Driven Devices, and Lab Scale Pump-Driven Devices. Bench Scale Vacuum-Driven Devices function by using a vacuum to draw liquid through the filtration membrane. Bench Scale Pressure-Driven Devices, on the other hand, use pressure to drive the liquid through the membrane. Similarly, Lab Scale Vacuum-Driven Devices and Lab Scale Pump-Driven Devices operate using vacuum and pump mechanisms respectively in order to facilitate the filtration process at a smaller scale.</p></p>

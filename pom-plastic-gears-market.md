<p><p>POM Plastic Gears are widely used in various industries due to their excellent properties such as low friction, high wear resistance, and self-lubrication. There are three main types of gears in the POM Plastic Gears market: Spur Gears, Helical Gears, and Worm Gears. Spur Gears have straight teeth and are ideal for low-speed and high-torque applications. Helical Gears have angled teeth, providing smoother and quieter operation at higher speeds. Worm Gears have a helical gear meshed with a worm gear, allowing for high gear ratios and torque while maintaining compact size.</p></p>

<p><p>Jewelry store management systems are available in two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are software solutions hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage their store data from any internet-connected device. On the other hand, on-premises systems are installed and maintained on the store's local servers, limiting access to the physical store location. Both market types offer their own set of advantages and considerations, allowing jewelry store owners to choose the option that best suits their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>Engineering Adhesives and Sealant Products are categorized based on their composition and properties. Water-soluble Type products are adhesive and sealants that can be dissolved in water for easy application and cleanup. Water Emulsion Type products are a mixture of water and adhesive/sealant compounds, providing a versatile and durable bonding solution. Solvent-based Type products utilize solvents for application and are known for their strong bonding capabilities. Solid-state Type products come in solid form and require curing to create a strong bond.</p></p>

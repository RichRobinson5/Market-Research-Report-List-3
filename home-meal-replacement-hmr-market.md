<p><p>The Home Meal Replacement (HMR) market is categorized into three types: Ready to Cook (RTC), Ready to Heat (RTH), and Ready to Eat (RTE). RTC meals require minimal preparation before cooking, often including pre-cut ingredients or marinated meats. RTH meals are fully cooked and only require reheating, typically done through methods like microwaving or oven heating. RTE meals are fully prepared and require no cooking or reheating, ready to be consumed straight from the package. These categories cater to different consumer preferences and convenience levels for home meal replacements.</p></p>

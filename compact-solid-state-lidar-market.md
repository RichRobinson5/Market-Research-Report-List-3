<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The compact solid-state LIDAR market encompasses three types of technologies: MEMS based scanning, phased array, and non-scanning flash. MEMS based scanning systems use micro-electro-mechanical systems to steer the laser beam and gather data through scanning movements. Phased array technology employs multiple solid-state emitters to steer the laser beam electronically, providing flexibility and rapid data acquisition. Non-scanning flash LIDAR emits laser pulses without any mechanical movement, relying on a large number of detectors to capture the reflected signals, enabling high-resolution imaging. These technologies offer diverse solutions for compact, solid-state LIDAR applications.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Industrial Smoke Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarm</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarm</li><li>Dual Sensor Smoke Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial smoke alarm market offers three types of smoke alarms: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and dual sensor smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms use a light source and sensor to detect smoke particles. Ionization smoke alarms use a small amount of radioactive material to ionize the air, while a sensor detects the change in electrical current caused by smoke particles. Dual sensor smoke alarms combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies to provide comprehensive smoke detection capabilities.</p></p>

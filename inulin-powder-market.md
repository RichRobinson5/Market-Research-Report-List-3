<p><p>Inulin powder market consists of two main types: chicory roots inulin and artichoke inulin. Chicory roots inulin is derived from the roots of chicory plant and is the most widely available inulin source. It is known for its tasteless and white powder form, making it suitable for various applications in food and beverage industries. On the other hand, artichoke inulin is obtained from the Jerusalem artichoke plant and offers similar functional properties as chicory inulin but with a slightly different taste profile. Both types are utilized for their dietary fiber benefits and prebiotic properties.</p></p>

<p><p>The Smartphone OLED Display market consists of two main types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) Display and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) Display. The AMOLED technology uses a thin-film transistor for each OLED pixel, resulting in better image quality, higher resolution, and faster response times. On the other hand, PMOLED technology utilizes a simple addressing scheme, suitable for smaller displays, with lower resolution and limited color options. These two types of OLED displays cater to different requirements in the smartphone industry, providing a range of options for manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>

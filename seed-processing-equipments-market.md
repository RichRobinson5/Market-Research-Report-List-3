<p><p>Seed processing equipment market is divided into several types such as cleaners, dryers, coaters, graders, separators, and polishers. Cleaners are used to remove impurities and foreign materials from seeds. Dryers are used to reduce the moisture content in seeds. Coaters are used to apply coatings or treatments to seeds. Graders are used to sort seeds based on size or weight. Separators are used to separate seeds based on specific characteristics. Polishers are used to give seeds a shiny appearance. Each type of equipment plays a crucial role in the seed processing industry.</p></p>

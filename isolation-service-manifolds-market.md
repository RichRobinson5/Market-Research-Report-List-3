<p><p>Isolation service manifolds are used in industrial applications to isolate pressure gauges, switches, transmitters, or other instruments. There are three main types of isolation service manifolds: 2 valve, 3 valve, and 5 valve. </p><p>2 valve manifolds have two valves for isolating pressure instruments, while 3 valve manifolds have an additional equalizing valve for accurate calibration and venting. </p><p>5 valve manifolds have two block valves, two equalizing valves, and a vent valve for precise control and maintenance of pressure instruments. Each type of manifold serves specific purposes based on the application requirements.</p></p>

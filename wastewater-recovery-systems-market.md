<p><ul><li>Reverse Osmosis</li><li>Membrane Filtration</li><li>Activated Carbon</li><li>Media Filtration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wastewater recovery systems market is comprised of several types including reverse osmosis, membrane filtration, activated carbon, and media filtration. Reverse osmosis involves forcing water through a semipermeable membrane to remove impurities. Membrane filtration uses a barrier that can block particles and microorganisms. Activated carbon uses a highly porous material to adsorb contaminants from the water. Media filtration involves passing water through a bed of granular media to remove suspended solids. These different types of systems are used to effectively treat and purify wastewater for reuse or safe discharge into the environment.</p></p>

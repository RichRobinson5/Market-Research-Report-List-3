<p><p>The animation collectibles market consists of various types of artwork related to animation. Reproduction art refers to copies of original artwork produced for commercial purposes. Concept art refers to the initial visual ideas and designs for characters, settings, and props in an animated project. Storyboards are sequential drawings that depict the narrative and action of an animated story. Each of these types holds value for collectors and enthusiasts in the animation industry.</p></p>

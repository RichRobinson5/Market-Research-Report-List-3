<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Semiconductor manufacturing equipment is divided into two types: front-end equipment and back-end equipment. Front-end equipment refers to the machines and tools used during the initial chip fabrication process, which includes processes like wafer fabrication, lithography, etching, and deposition. It plays a crucial role in forming the electronic components on the semiconductor wafer. On the other hand, back-end equipment is used for packaging and testing the finished semiconductor chips. It involves processes like assembly, bonding, encapsulation, and quality testing before the chips are ready for use.</p></p>

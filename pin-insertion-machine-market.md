<p><p>The pin insertion machine market is divided into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operators to manually insert pins into products. Semi-automatic machines have some automated functions but still require manual intervention for placing the pins. Fully automatic machines are highly automated and can insert pins without any human intervention. These machines are equipped with advanced technology, such as sensors, robotics, and computer control systems, allowing them to perform the insertion process efficiently and accurately. The type of machine chosen depends on the specific requirements and production volume of the industry.</p></p>

<p><p>A military laser rangefinder is a device used by armed forces to accurately determine the distance between the observer and a target using laser technology. These technologies further enhance the capabilities of the rangefinders and provide additional situational awareness to military personnel.</p><p>Another trend is the development of lightweight and compact laser rangefinders.

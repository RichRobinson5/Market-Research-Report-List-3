<p><p>Almond oil is available in two market types; sweet almond oil and bitter almond oil. Sweet almond oil is derived from edible almonds, and it has a mild, nutty flavor. It is widely used in cooking, skincare, and haircare products. On the other hand, bitter almond oil is extracted from bitter almonds, which contain amygdalin, a compound that converts into toxic cyanide. Therefore, bitter almond oil is mainly used in the production of perfumes, cosmetics, and other non-edible applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The whey protein ingredient market consists of three main types: whey protein concentrate, whey protein isolate, and hydrolyzed whey protein. </p><p>Whey protein concentrate is the most common form, obtained by removing water from the whey and contains around 70-80% protein. Whey protein isolate undergoes further processing, resulting in a higher protein content of around 90%. Hydrolyzed whey protein goes through enzymatic hydrolysis, breaking down the proteins into smaller peptides, making it easier to digest. These different types cater to varying consumer needs in terms of protein content and digestion.</p></p>

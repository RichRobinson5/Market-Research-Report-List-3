<p><strong>The Polyimide Insulating Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Polyimide Tube</li><li>Spiral Wound Polyimide Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide insulating tubes come in two main types: Seamless polyimide tubes and spiral wound polyimide tubes. Seamless polyimide tubes are manufactured without any seams or joints, providing a continuous and uniform insulation solution. Spiral wound polyimide tubes, on the other hand, are made by winding a polyimide film around a mandrel, creating a strong and durable insulating tube. Both types of tubes are used in various industries for their excellent thermal and electrical insulation properties.</p></p>

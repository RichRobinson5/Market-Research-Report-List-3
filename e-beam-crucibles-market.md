<p><p>E-Beam crucibles come in various shapes based on their intended use in the deposition process. Round crucibles are typically used for general-purpose evaporation applications due to their even heat distribution and minimal material wastage. Cone-shaped crucibles are commonly used for materials that require higher temperatures for sublimation or evaporation. They are designed to provide better control over the direction of the vapor flux and have a higher material capacity compared to round crucibles. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the deposition process.</p></p>

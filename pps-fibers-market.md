<p><p>PPS fibers are available in two main market types: PPS filaments and PPS staple fibers. PPS filaments are long, continuous fibers that are typically used in applications requiring high strength and stability. On the other hand, PPS staple fibers are shorter fibers that are often blended with other materials to enhance properties such as heat and chemical resistance. Both types of PPS fibers are in high demand in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications due to their superior performance characteristics.</p></p>

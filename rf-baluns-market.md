<p><p>RF Baluns are devices used in radio frequency systems to convert between unbalanced and balanced signals. </p><p>Unbalanced to Balanced baluns take an unbalanced input signal and convert it to a balanced output signal. This is commonly used when connecting devices with unbalanced outputs, such as coaxial cables, to devices with balanced inputs, such as twisted pair cables.</p><p>Balanced to Balanced baluns take a balanced input signal and convert it to another balanced output signal. This type of balun is used when connecting devices with balanced outputs to other devices with balanced inputs.</p><p>Unbalanced to Unbalanced baluns, on the other hand, simply pass an unbalanced input signal to an unbalanced output without any conversion.</p></p>

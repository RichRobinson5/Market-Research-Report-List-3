<p><ul><li>Manual Cleaning</li><li>Automatic Cleaning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical device cleaning market includes two types: manual cleaning and automatic cleaning. Manual cleaning involves physically washing and scrubbing medical devices using brushes, detergents, and water. This method requires human intervention and is time-consuming. On the other hand, automatic cleaning uses specialized machines that employ various cleaning techniques such as ultrasonic cleaning, high-pressure spraying, and hot air drying. This type of cleaning is efficient and saves time. Both manual and automatic cleaning methods play crucial roles in maintaining the cleanliness and functionality of medical devices.</p></p>

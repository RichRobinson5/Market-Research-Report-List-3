<p><strong>The Portable Turbidity Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scattered Light Turbidimeter</li><li>Transmitted Light Turbidimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Turbidity Analyzer market consists of two main types: the Scattered Light Turbidimeter and the Transmitted Light Turbidimeter. A Scattered Light Turbidimeter measures the amount of light scattered by suspended particles in a sample to determine turbidity. It is commonly used for water quality monitoring in various industries. On the other hand, a Transmitted Light Turbidimeter measures the amount of light transmitted through a sample to determine turbidity. It is often used for applications requiring higher precision and sensitivity, such as research laboratories and environmental monitoring agencies.</p></p>

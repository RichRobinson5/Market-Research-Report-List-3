<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Bicycle bearing market includes three main types: loose ball bearings, retainer bearings, and sealed cartridge bearings. Loose ball bearings are individual balls placed between two bearing races, allowing smooth rotation. Retainer bearings consist of a retainer that holds balls in position within the bearing races. Sealed cartridge bearings are pre-assembled units containing ball bearings and are sealed within a cartridge, providing protection against dirt and moisture. These different market types cater to varying customer preferences and requirements for a smooth and reliable cycling experience.</p></p>

<p><p>Rectangular Ground Modules are commonly classified into three market types based on the way the electrical connection is made: Crimped Contact, PCB Contact, and Others. Crimped Contact modules involve using a crimping tool to make a secure connection with the wire. PCB Contact modules are directly soldered onto a printed circuit board for a permanent connection. Other types of modules may include snap-on connectors or screw terminals for varying installation requirements. Each market type offers a unique solution for grounding applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The medium voltage plastic film capacitors market comprises two main types: polyester film capacitors and metallized film capacitors. Polyester film capacitors are made of a thin layer of polyester film that is sandwiched between two metal electrodes. These capacitors offer good insulation properties and are widely used in various applications. On the other hand, metallized film capacitors have a thin layer of metal deposited on the plastic film, which enhances their voltage handling capabilities. These capacitors are known for their accuracy, long life, and high reliability in applications such as power electronics and renewable energy systems.</p></p>

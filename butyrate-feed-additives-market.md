<p><strong>The Butyrate Feed Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sodium Butyrate</li><li>Calcium Butyrate</li><li>Tributyrins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Butyrate feed additives are commonly used in animal nutrition to improve gut health, promote growth, and enhance feed efficiency. Three main types of butyrate feed additives include sodium butyrate, calcium butyrate, and tributyrins. Sodium butyrate is known for its anti-inflammatory properties and ability to improve gut health. Calcium butyrate is preferred for its slow release characteristics and benefits for gastrointestinal health. Tributyrins are a more concentrated form of butyrate and are often used for their strong antimicrobial effects in the animal gut.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Self-Organizing Network Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>D-SON</li><li>C-SON</li><li>Hybrid SON</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-Organizing Network (SON) market consists of three main types: Distributed SON (D-SON), Centralized SON (C-SON), and Hybrid SON. D-SON involves individual cells making autonomous decisions. C-SON centralizes decision-making at a higher level, allowing for better coordination among cells. Hybrid SON combines features of both D-SON and C-SON, offering a flexible and efficient solution for network optimization. Each type has its advantages and is suitable for different network configurations and requirements, depending on the specific needs of the operator.</p></p>

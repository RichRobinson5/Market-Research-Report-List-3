<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Distributed Strain Sensor market can be categorized into two types based on the delivery mechanism. The first type is based on Rayleigh Backscatter, where strain measurements are achieved by detecting the changes in the light wavelength due to strain applied to the optical fiber. The second type is based on Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBGs), where strain measurements are obtained by analyzing the changes in the Bragg wavelength. Both methods are widely used in the market to provide accurate and efficient distributed strain sensing solutions.</p></p>

<p><p>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) Systems are medical devices used to treat patients with heart failure. There are two main types of CRT systems: Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillators (CRT-Ds) and Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Pacemakers (CRT-Ps). CRT-Ds deliver electric shocks to treat life-threatening arrhythmias, while CRT-Ps deliver timed electrical pulses to help coordinate the contractions of the heart's chambers. These devices are designed to improve the overall function of the heart and reduce symptoms of heart failure.</p></p>

<p><p>Automated industrial drones refer to unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) that are designed for various industrial applications. These drones can be classified into three market types: fixed wing, multiple rotor, and hybrid wing. Fixed wing drones resemble mini airplanes, enabling them to cover large distances efficiently. Multiple rotor drones, such as quadcopters, provide excellent maneuverability and are capable of hovering and vertical take-offs and landings. Hybrid wing drones combine the features of both fixed wing and multiple rotor drones, allowing for longer flight times and versatile operations.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Laminating Adhesives for Industrial Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based Laminating Adhesives</li><li>Solventless Laminating Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminating adhesives for the industrial market come in two main types: solvent-based and solventless. Solvent-based adhesives contain volatile organic compounds that must evaporate before bonding, while solventless adhesives cure through chemical reactions without the need for a solvent carrier. Solvent-based adhesives are favored for their fast cure times and high bond strength, while solventless adhesives are valued for their environmental friendliness and lack of volatile emissions. Both types are used widely in the industrial market for various applications.</p></p>

Glucose lactate analyzers, on the other hand, measure both glucose and lactate levels simultaneously in a single sample. These analyzers are valuable tools for monitoring exercise intensity, identifying lactate thresholds, and assessing metabolic disorders. They offer convenience, accuracy, and quick results, making them popular in sports medicine and clinical settings.</p></p>

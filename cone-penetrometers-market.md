<p><p>The cone penetrometers market includes two types: hand operated static cone penetrometer and self-start static cone penetrometer. The hand operated static cone penetrometer consists of a cone-shaped probe that is manually pushed into the ground to measure soil strength and consistency. On the other hand, the self-start static cone penetrometer can automatically penetrate the ground by using its own weight and does not require manual intervention. Both types are used for soil investigation and geotechnical engineering purposes, providing valuable data on soil properties.</p></p>

<p><p>Vertical decanter centrifuges are used for solid-liquid separation in various industries. There are two types of vertical decanter centrifuges: two-phase decanter centrifuges and three-phase decanter centrifuges. </p><p>A two-phase decanter centrifuge separates a mixture of solid and liquid into two distinct phases, such as liquid and solids. This is typically used for applications where only the solid and liquid fractions need to be separated.</p><p>On the other hand, a three-phase decanter centrifuge separates a mixture of solid, liquid, and oil into three distinct phases. This type of centrifuge is used when all three phases need to be separated, such as in the processing of wastewater or the extraction of oil from sludge.</p><p>Both types of vertical decanter centrifuges play a crucial role in achieving efficient separation of different phases in industrial processes.</p></p>

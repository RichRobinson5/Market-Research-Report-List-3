<p><p>The air cleaner filters market can be classified into several types. Pre filters are commonly used as the initial line of defense to catch large particles such as dust and pet hair. Sub-HEPA filters are designed to capture smaller particles and improve the efficiency of the air cleaner. Secondary filters help remove odors and harmful gases from the air. HEPA (High Efficiency Particulate Air) and ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters are highly effective in eliminating allergens and microscopic particles. Other types include activated carbon filters, electrostatic filters, and UV filters.</p></p>

<p><p>Dynamometer products and services are used for measuring and evaluating the performance of engines and motors. Absorption dynamometer products and services involve absorbing energy produced by the tested equipment to measure its output. Transmission dynamometer products and services measure power generated by the equipment by transmitting it to a load via a driveline. Motoring market dynamometers allow the testing of motors and engines by providing a load that mimics real-world conditions, allowing for performance analysis without the need for an external power source.</p></p>

In the manual market, users have complete control over the entire brewing process, requiring them to manually operate the machine to control things like water pressure and extraction time. Semi-automatic machines provide a balance between manual control and convenience, as they have automated features for water temperature and pressure but still require the user to start and stop the brewing process. Fully-automatic machines handle all aspects of brewing with the push of a button, automatically controlling water flow, temperature, pressure, and extraction time.</p></p>

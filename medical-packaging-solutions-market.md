<p><strong>The Medical Packaging Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Packaging</li><li>Secondary Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical packaging solutions market can be categorized into primary packaging and secondary packaging. Primary packaging refers to the initial layer of packaging in direct contact with the medical product, such as vials or blister packs. It ensures product safety, integrity, and stability. Secondary packaging, on the other hand, refers to the outer layer that contains the primary packaging, such as bottles, cartons, or pouches. It provides additional protection, information, and branding. Both primary and secondary packaging play crucial roles in ensuring the quality and safety of medical products throughout their supply chain.</p></p>

<p><p>A tailbone cushion, also known as a coccyx cushion, is a medical device designed to relieve pressure on the coccyx or tailbone. This cushion is commonly used by individuals suffering from conditions like tailbone injuries, fractures, coccydynia, hemorrhoids, pregnancy-related pain, and those who need to sit for long periods.</p><p>The tailbone cushion market is projected to witness significant growth at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period.

<p><p>The market for cold and flu drugs can be classified into various types. Antihistamines help relieve symptoms such as sneezing and runny nose by blocking the effects of histamine. Expectorants work by loosening mucus and phlegm, aiding in their expulsion from the respiratory tract. Bronchodilators help relax and open up airways, making breathing easier. Decongestants reduce nasal congestion by constricting blood vessels in the nasal passages. Antibiotics are used to treat bacterial infections that may arise as complications. Other types include herbal remedies, cough suppressants, and pain relievers.</p></p>

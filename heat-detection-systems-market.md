<p><p>Heat detection systems are classified into four types: Heat Detector Type, Ionization Smoke Detector Type, Photoelectric Smoke Detector Type, and Ionization/Photoelectric Smoke Detector Type. Heat Detector Type systems detect changes in temperature and provide early warning for fire incidents. Ionization Smoke Detector Type systems use a small amount of radioactive material to detect smoke particles generated by fires. Photoelectric Smoke Detector Type systems use a light sensor to detect smoke particles. Ionization/Photoelectric Smoke Detector Type systems combine both ionization and photoelectric technologies for enhanced fire detection capabilities.</p></p>

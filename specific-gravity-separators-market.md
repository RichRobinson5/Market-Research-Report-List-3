<p><p>Specific gravity separators are used in various industries to separate different types of materials based on the difference in their densities. Air blow type specific gravity separators rely on the application of an upward air flow to separate lighter materials from heavier ones. On the other hand, air suction type specific gravity separators use a downward air flow to remove heavier materials from lighter ones. Both types of separators operate on the principle of exploiting the difference in specific gravity of materials to achieve efficient separation and sorting.</p></p>

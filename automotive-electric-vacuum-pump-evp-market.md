<p><p>The Automotive Electric Vacuum Pump (EVP) market includes three main types: diaphragm type, leaf type, and swing piston type. Diaphragm type EVPs use a flexible diaphragm to create vacuum pressure. Leaf type EVPs utilize a series of leaf springs to pump air. Swing piston type EVPs have a swinging piston mechanism to generate vacuum. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different automotive applications based on their design and functionality.</p></p>

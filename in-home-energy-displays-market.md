<p><p>In-Home Energy Displays (IHD) Market Types refer to the different technologies used to display real-time energy consumption data to consumers. ZigBee IHDs utilize ZigBee wireless communication protocol to connect with smart meters and display energy usage information. Non-AMI IHDs do not require advanced metering infrastructure (AMI) and can connect to traditional electricity meters to provide consumption data. Both types of IHDs help consumers monitor and manage their energy usage, promoting energy efficiency and cost savings.</p></p>

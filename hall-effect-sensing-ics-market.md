<p><p>Hall Effect Sensing ICs are electronic devices that detect the presence and strength of magnetic fields. Digital Output Hall Effect Sensing ICs provide binary output signals (usually high or low) based on the presence or absence of a magnetic field. These ICs are commonly used in applications that require simple on/off detection. On the other hand, Analog Output Hall Effect Sensing ICs provide continuous output signals proportional to the magnetic field intensity. These ICs are used in applications that require precise measurement and control of magnetic fields.</p></p>

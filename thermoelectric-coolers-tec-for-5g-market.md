<p><p>Thermoelectric Coolers (TEC) are used for cooling applications in the 5G market. There are two types: Single Stage Module and Multiple Modules. Single Stage Module TECs have one hot side and one cold side, offering a single level of cooling. Multiple Modules TECs, on the other hand, consist of multiple stages with cascaded thermoelectric elements, providing higher levels of cooling. Each module has its own cold side and hot side. Both types are used to regulate temperatures in devices and systems to ensure efficient operation in the 5G market.</p></p>

Pneumatic Drills are powered by compressed air and provide high torque capabilities. Electric Drills are powered by electricity and offer consistent performance. Battery-powered Drills are portable and convenient for use in surgical settings where access to electricity may be limited. Each type of drill caters to different needs and preferences in the medical micro surgical field, providing versatility and efficiency in various procedures.</p></p>

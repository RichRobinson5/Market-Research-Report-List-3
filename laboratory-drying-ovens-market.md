<p><p>The laboratory drying ovens market consists of various types of ovens, including forced air ovens, vacuum ovens, and gravity convection ovens. Forced air ovens utilize a fan for efficient heat distribution, ensuring uniform drying of samples. Vacuum ovens operate under reduced pressure, which lowers the boiling point, making them suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Gravity convection ovens rely on natural convection for heat circulation. These ovens are commonly used in laboratories for drying, sterilizing, and other heat treatment processes, catering to different needs based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>

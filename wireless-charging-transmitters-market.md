<p><p>Wireless charging transmitters are devices that enable the transfer of power from a power source to a recipient device without the need for physical connectors. There are two main types of wireless charging transmitters in the market: single coil and multi coil. Single coil transmitters consist of a single coil and are generally smaller and less expensive. On the other hand, multi coil transmitters have multiple coils, allowing for a larger charging area and the ability to charge multiple devices simultaneously. They are typically larger and pricier than single coil transmitters.</p></p>

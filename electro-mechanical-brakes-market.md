<p><strong>The Electro-mechanical Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Face Brake</li><li>Power off Brake</li><li>Particle Brake</li><li>Hysteresis Power Brake</li><li>Multiple Disk Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro-mechanical brakes can be categorized into different types based on their functionality. Single Face Brakes are used for applications where space is limited, while Power-off Brakes are designed to hold loads in place when power is cut off. Particle Brakes provide adjustable torque by controlling the flow of magnetic particles. Hysteresis Power Brakes rely on hysteresis to dissipate heat and generate braking force. Multiple Disk Brakes offer high torque capacity and are commonly used in heavy-duty applications. Each type has specific advantages and is chosen based on the requirements of the application.</p></p>

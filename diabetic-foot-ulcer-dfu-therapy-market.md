<p><p>Diabetic Foot Ulcer (DFU) Therapy involves the treatment and management of ulcers that occur on the feet of diabetic patients. DFU therapy typically involves a combination of wound care, infection control, offloading pressure from the affected area, and managing blood sugar levels.</p><p>The Diabetic Foot Ulcer (DFU) Therapy Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period.

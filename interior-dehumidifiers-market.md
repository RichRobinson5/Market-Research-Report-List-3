<p><p>The interior dehumidifiers market comprises two types: mechanical dehumidifiers and electronic dehumidifiers. Mechanical dehumidifiers function by drawing in air, cooling it down, and extracting moisture through condensation. These units are equipped with a compressor and a refrigerant to accomplish this task. On the other hand, electronic dehumidifiers work by using an electric fan to blow air onto a refrigerated surface, causing the moisture to condense. These types of dehumidifiers are generally smaller and quieter in operation compared to mechanical ones.</p></p>

<p><p>The Toric Soft Contact Lenses market comprises two main types: Conventional Hydrogel Material and Highly Breathable Silicone Hydrogel. Conventional Hydrogel Material lenses are made of a hydrogel material that provides average breathability and moisture retention. On the other hand, Highly Breathable Silicone Hydrogel lenses are manufactured using a silicone hydrogel material, which allows for increased oxygen transmission to the cornea and better moisture retention. These lenses are designed specifically for individuals with astigmatism and offer enhanced comfort, longer wear time, and clearer vision.</p></p>

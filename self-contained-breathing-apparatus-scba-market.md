<p><p>Self Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) is a device used by firefighters and other emergency responders to provide breathable air in hazardous environments. There are two main types of SCBA: Open-Circuit SCBA, which releases exhaled air into the environment, and Closed-Circuit SCBA, which recirculates exhaled air after removing carbon dioxide. Open-Circuit SCBA is more common and cost-effective, while Closed-Circuit SCBA is typically used in situations where air supply is limited or where contamination is a concern.</p></p>
